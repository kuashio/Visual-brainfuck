About Visual brainfuck

Visual brainfuck v1.2.0 was developed by Eduardo Corpeńo (Kuashio) mostly in 2003 while he was working for a very patient guy who allowed him to learn to code in Borland C++ Builder 6 by making a project of his own.


Ever since, and until 2011, Kuashio had been chased and tormented by an relentless demon who didn't allow him to sleep well and look at the mirror without guilt. That demon is called procrastination. Finish your stuff, people!


In 2011 Kuashio got all his code together and had to take on where he left the project back in 2003. He added the compiler, this help file and the installer.


For comments, visit my blog: kuashio.blogspot.com.
